Selecting e-mail client

You have the following options:

  1. Use M2, Opera's built-in e-mail client (recommended)
  2. Use the default e-mail client on your computer if you already have an account and you do not want to switch to M2
  3. Use a specific e-mail client (recommended for advanced users only)
